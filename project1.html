College Attendance Portal

Project Overview

The College Attendance Portal is a secure desktop-based application designed to simplify and automate student attendance management. It enables faculty and administrators to accurately mark, track, and analyze attendance records using a centralized database.

Objective

  • Reduces manual effort
  • Improves accuracy
  • Provides real-time attendance insights
